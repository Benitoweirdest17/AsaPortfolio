My Favorite Place

Baguio City

Baguio was discovered way back in 1572 by Juan Salcedo.
It was established as a hill station in 1900 at Ibaloy Village known as Kafagway.
In 1903, an American architect named Daniel Hudson Burnham had an idea to turn it into a city.

Baguio became my favorite place since when I was 6 because I used to make the
best memories of my life there. Living there used to be my dream;
unfortunately, my mom didn't allow me to live and study there alone.
However, it is still a privilege for me to go back there every summer or
between the months of January and February. Baguio will always have a special place in my heart.
